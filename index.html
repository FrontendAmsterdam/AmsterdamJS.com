AmsterdamJS, the largest and
longest-running JavaScript meetup in town

We are organizing four events per year, accessible to all and free of charge. Events take place on Thursday evenings and are hosted in varying venues provided by friends of the community. Each event will feature a couple of talks by members or guests of the community. Speakers are carefully curated and vetted by the organizing team to guarantee top notch quality. Before and after the talks there will be plenty of opportunity to have a drink, meet old friends and make new ones.

location

Natura Artis Magistra

It is the oldest zoo in the Netherlands and one of the oldest zoos of mainland Europe. Artis Royal Zoo is not just a zoo, it also contains an aquarium and a planetarium. Artis also has an arboretum and a fairly large art collection. Artis contains 27 monumental buildings, most of which are used as enclosures for the animals, making Artis a unique cultural heritage of the 19th century.
